# 🏥 VitalID
**Smart Emergency Health Identity & Record Exchange Platform**

VitalID gives every patient a unique ID and QR code so authorized hospitals can instantly access critical medical records — even in emergencies when the patient is unconscious.

---

## 🚨 Problem
Hospitals can't access a patient's history when they visit a new facility. Missing allergy, medication, or condition data delays treatment and risks lives.

## 💡 Solution
A secure platform where patients register once and get a unique health ID. Verified hospitals scan the QR to instantly view a complete medical summary — with patient consent, or via emergency override.

---

## ✨ Features
- 🪪 Unique Patient ID + QR Code
- 🚨 Emergency access mode (for unconscious patients)
- 🔐 Consent-based record sharing
- 🏥 Verified hospital-only access
- 🤖 AI alerts for drug conflicts & allergies
- 📋 Full audit logs
